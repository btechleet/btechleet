1. What is Gujarat DDCET?

The **Diploma to Degree Common Entrance Test (DDCET)** is the official entrance test administered by Gujarat for diploma holders. The **Admission Committee for Professional Courses (ACPC), Gujarat**, regulates all professional degree counseling procedures based on DDCET ranks.

DDCET is the mandatory gate to secure direct second-year (third semester) admissions in government-run, grant-in-aid, and self-financed engineering institutions across Gujarat affiliated with **Gujarat Technological University (GTU)** or prominent state private universities.

2. Eligibility Guidelines

Candidates must satisfy the following terms to qualify for DDCET:

  • Academic Qualification: Must have passed an AICTE-approved 3-year minimum Diploma course in Engineering or Technology with at least 45% marks (40% for candidates belonging to SC/ST/SEBC categories).
  • ACPC Seat Reservation: Candidates must hold valid domicile or resident certificates in Gujarat to secure government-subsidized seats. A specific allocation of seats is set aside for other-state students under separate quotas.
  • No Age Restrictions: There is no official upper age limit to sit for DDCET.

3. Exam Pattern & Core Sections

DDCET is an online Computer Based Test (CBT) consisting of multiple-choice questions.

4. Top Engineering Colleges accepts DDCET in Gujarat

Earning a high percentile rank in DDCET unlocks seats in top-tier institutions:

  1. LDCE Ahmedabad (L.D. College of Engineering) — Outstanding placement profile, most sought-after.
  2. MSU Baroda (Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda) — Highly reputed faculty, historic brand value.
  3. VGEC Chandkheda (Vishwakarma Government Engineering College) — Modern state university setup.
  4. BVM Engineering College, Vallabh Vidyanagar — Excellent historic engineering reputation.
  5. GEC Gandhinagar & GEC Surat — Leading state colleges for computing and electronics.

5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Does DDCET have negative marking?
No. Standard DDCET guides state that there is no negative penalty for wrong answers. Attempting all questions is highly recommended.
What is the role of ACPC in lateral admissions?
ACPC is the central governing body in Gujarat. They verify documents, release merit lists based on DDCET percentile ranks, and coordinate the complete online college allocation rounds.
