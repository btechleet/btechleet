1. What is SLIET Lateral Entry (SET-III)?

**Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering and Technology (SLIET)** is a premium Centrally Funded Technical Institute (CFTI) established by the Government of India in Longowal, Sangrur, Punjab. It holds a distinct university status and offers a direct lateral entry entrance program named **SET-III (SLIET Entrance Test-III)**.

This national-level examination enables candidates holding a 3-year State Board Diploma in Engineering to directly enter the 2nd year (3rd semester) of prestigious 4-year B.Tech degree programs under specialized CFTI seat matrices.

2. Eligibility Criteria

Candidates seeking admissions via the SET-III path must fulfill the official parameters:

  • Academic Qualification: Must have passed a 3-year Diploma course in any discipline of Engineering or Technology from a recognized State Board of Technical Education with at least 50% aggregate marks (45% for reserved SC/ST candidates).
  • Age Boundaries: Candidates must not exceed 30 years of age (35 years for SC/ST/PH categories) on July 1 of the admission year.
  • Domain Fitment: Admissions are open to all students across India. Both Punjab quota and All-India open seat quotas are maintained.

3. SET-III Exam Syllabus and Weights

The exam has 150 MCQs. There is negative marking. Here is the official subject composition:

Subject Questions Key Sub-Topics
General Engineering 50 MCQs Basic Electrical Engineering, Mechanics, Computer Applications, Graphics
Physics & Chemistry 30 MCQs Modern Physics, Electrostatics, Organic Chemistry, Environmental Science
Mathematics 30 MCQs Matrices, Determinants, Vector Algebra, Co-ordinate Geometry, Calculus
Specialized Branch Subject 40 MCQs Subject pertaining strictly to candidate's diploma domain (CSE/EE/ME/ECE)

4. Top Engineering Branches at SLIET Longowal

SLIET offers world-class lab and training infrastructures across core and modern engineering branches:

  • Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Electrical Engineering (EE)
  • Mechanical Engineering (Specialization: Manufacturing, Welding, or Thermal)
  • Chemical Engineering & Food Technology

5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are internal SLIET diploma students given a preference?
Yes. Under the unique certificate-diploma-degree modular system at SLIET, a specific percentage of B.Tech seats are reserved for internal SLIET diploma certificate holders via the vertical promotion criteria.
Where is the exam center located?
SET-III is conducted online/offline at designated centers across multiple key North India cities, including Chandigarh, Sangrur, Ludhiana, Delhi, and Jammu.
