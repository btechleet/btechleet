1. What is TG ECET?

The **Telangana State Engineering Common Entrance Test (TG ECET)** is conducted annually by **Osmania University** on behalf of the **Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TGCHE)**. It is a highly competitive examination specifically tailored for Diploma holders in Engineering, Technology, Pharmacy, and BSc (Mathematics) graduates.

Success in TG ECET enables candidates to gain direct admission into the 2nd year (3rd semester) of B.Tech degree programs in government, aided, and private engineering universities across Telangana.

2. Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must fulfill the following parameters to qualify for TG ECET:

  • Academic Qualification: Must have obtained a Diploma in Engineering and Technology from the State Board of Technical Education of Telangana or an equivalent board with at least 45% aggregate marks (40% for candidates belonging to reserved SC/ST/BC categories).
  • Nativity: Candidates should belong to the state of Telangana / Andhra Pradesh and satisfy the local status requirements as laid down in the state admission regulations.
  • No Age Restrictions: There is no official maximum age limit.

3. Exam Pattern & Syllabus Weights

TG ECET is a Computer Based Test (CBT) containing **200 Multiple Choice Questions** (MCQs) for 200 total marks. The duration is 3 hours. The marking scheme is +1 mark for a correct response and **no negative marking**.

4. Top Engineering Colleges accepts TG ECET in Telangana

The leading engineering campuses accepting TG ECET ranks in Hyderabad are:

  1. JNTU Hyderabad College of Engineering — Prestigious government university campus, highly sought after.
  2. Osmania University College of Engineering (OUCE) — Legendary academic ranking, top placement cell.
  3. CBIT Hyderabad (Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology) — Premier private institution with top computing placements.
  4. Vasavi College of Engineering, Hyderabad — Reputed autonomous engineering campus.
  5. VNR VJIET Hyderabad (Vignana Jyothi Institute) — Modern laboratory infrastructures and high ranking.

5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is there negative marking in TG ECET?
No. Osmania University does not apply negative marking in TG ECET. Students should answer all 200 questions to maximize their score potential.
Are AP ECET students eligible for TG ECET counseling?
Yes. Under the local/non-local status provisions, a specific percentage of unreserved seats are open to both Telangana and Andhra Pradesh lateral entry candidates based on their ranks.
