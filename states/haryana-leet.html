1. What is Haryana B.Tech Lateral Entry (HSTES)?

In Haryana, B.Tech Lateral Entry admissions are administered centrally by the **Haryana State Technical Education Society (HSTES)**, Panchkula.

Admissions into the 2nd year (3rd semester) of 4-year B.Tech courses are conducted on the basis of **normalized Merit Marks computed from the candidate's 3-year Diploma percentage**. In recent sessions, Haryana Technical Board shifted from offline written exams to a centralized Online Merit-Based Counseling process.

2. Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must fulfill the official parameters to participate in HSTES counseling:

  • Academic Qualification: Passed 3-year minimum Diploma exam from an AICTE-approved institution with at least 45% aggregate marks (40% in case of candidates belonging to SC/ST categories).
  • HSTES Seat Reservation: 85% of total seats are reserved strictly for Haryana Resident candidates. The remaining 15% are open to candidates across all Indian states under the All-India open category.
  • Online Merit normalization: Merit ranks are released on the HSTES portal after physical or online document verification checks.

3. HSTES Admissions Stages

Milestone Step HSTES Procedure Details
1. Online Registration Candidates register at techadmissionshry.gov.in and upload semester diploma marksheets.
2. Rank Generation HSTES normalized lists are generated. Tier ranks are calculated strictly from academic aggregate marks.
3. Online Choice Filing Students login to fill preferred branches and college lockings. YMCA, DCRUST and GJU are top priorities.
4. Reporting & Admission Seat allocations are released in two main rounds, followed by physical reporting and fee submission.

4. Top Engineering Colleges in Haryana for Lateral Entry

Haryana hosts multiple prestigious state universities highly coveted by engineering candidates:

  1. J. C. Bose UST (YMCA), Faridabad — Formerly YMCA University. Globally famous for computer science coding placements. A highly active alumni base.
  2. DCRUST Murthal (Deenbandhu Chhotu Ram University of Science & Technology) — Excellent infrastructure, high core and IT placements, low fee structures.
  3. GJU Hisar (Guru Jambheshwar University of Science & Technology) — Top-tier computing resources and massive campus.
  4. UIET Kurukshetra University — Meticulous curriculum, active campus placements.
  5. UIET Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU), Rohtak — Highly respected state campus.

YMCA Faridabad Cutoff Reality: General merit ranks under **HSTES Rank #1 to #150** typically secure Computer Science (CSE) or Information Technology (IT) seats at YMCA Faridabad. Preparing a high diploma GPA is vital!

5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is there an offline entrance test for Haryana LEET?
No. For B.Tech lateral entry admissions, HSTES has fully transitioned to merit-based lists generated directly from your cumulative engineering diploma marksheet percentage. No separate written exam is conducted.
Is a domicile certificate compulsory for all applicants?
A Haryana Resident Certificate is mandatory to compete for the 85% Haryana State Quota seats. Other state students can compete under the 15% All-India Quota pool.
