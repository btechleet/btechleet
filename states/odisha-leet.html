1. OJEE Lateral Entry Overview

The **Odisha Joint Entrance Examination (OJEE)** conducts specialized entrance tests for admissions into the 2nd year (3rd Semester) of B.Tech (Lateral Entry) programs across Odisha. This pathway is designed specifically for students holding a 3-year Diploma in Engineering or technology branches.

Admissions are conducted through centralized counseling based on OJEE Lateral Entry ranks. The government of Odisha ensures 10% of total sanctioned intake seats in engineering colleges are reserved strictly for lateral entry students, plus any unfilled seats from the prior academic session.

2. Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must satisfy the following parameters to qualify for OJEE LE admissions:

  • Academic Qualification: Must have passed a 3-year minimum Diploma exam from an AICTE-approved institution or State Council of Technical Education (SCTE&VT) with at least 45% aggregate marks (40% for SC/ST categories) in appropriate engineering branches.
  • Residency: While open to other states, government college seats (like OUTR, VSSUT, IGIT) are reserved strictly for permanent residents of Odisha holding valid residential certificates.
  • Age Limit: There is no upper age limit for lateral entry admissions in Odisha.

3. Exam Pattern & Syllabus

The OJEE LE entrance consists of a Computer Based Test (CBT) containing **120 Multiple Choice Questions** (MCQs) carrying 4 marks each. Negative marking of -1 is active for incorrect answers.

Subject Section No. of MCQs Topics Covered

4. Top Engineering Colleges in Odisha accepting OJEE LE

Securing a high rank in OJEE LE unlocks admissions into prestigious government state universities:

  1. OUTR Bhubaneswar (Odisha University of Technology and Research) — Highly coveted for Computer Science and Core branches.
  2. VSSUT Burla (Veer Surendra Sai University of Technology) — Extremely reputed, oldest government engineering college.
  3. IGIT Sarang (Indira Gandhi Institute of Technology) — Excellent placement record for core branches.
  4. CET Bhubaneswar (College of Engineering and Technology) — Now part of OUTR.
  5. GCE Keonjhar & GCE Kalahandi — Quality government college infrastructures.

5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can BSc graduates apply for OJEE Lateral Entry?
Yes. BSc graduates who have completed their degree with Mathematics in 12th standard are eligible for lateral entry, but priority is given to Diploma holders during branch allocation.
Is there a negative marking in the OJEE LE exam?
Yes. OJEE follows a +4 and -1 marking scheme. Make sure to skip questions you are uncertain about to preserve your score.
How are seats allocated in VSSUT Burla?
Seats in VSSUT are allocated strictly via OJEE counseling based on your category rank and preferences filled. Top 100 general ranks usually secure CS/IT seats.
