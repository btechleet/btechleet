1. What is Bihar BCECE-LE?

The **Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB)** conducts the **BCECE-LE (Lateral Entry)** entrance exam every year. This specialized examination is the exclusive channel for 3-year Polytechnic Diploma in Engineering holders to gain direct second-year (third semester) admissions into 4-year B.Tech programs in Bihar government and private engineering institutes.

Under Bihar's Department of Science, Technology and Technical Education (DST), the state hosts over 38 government engineering colleges—one in each district—all affiliated with **Bihar Engineering University (BEU), Patna**.

2. Eligibility Criteria

To seek government seat counseling under BCECE-LE, candidates must fulfill the following:

  • Academic Qualification: Must have passed a 3-year minimum Diploma course in Engineering or Technology from a recognized State Council (SBTE Bihar or equivalent) with at least 45% aggregate marks (40% for candidates belonging to SC/ST categories).
  • Nativity: Government college seat allocations are restricted strictly to candidates whose parents are permanent residents of Bihar.
  • Age Boundaries: There is no upper or lower age limit for applying to BCECE-LE.

3. Exam Pattern & Syllabus Weights

The BCECE-LE offline examination comprises a single paper containing **150 Multiple Choice Questions** (MCQs) for 600 total marks. The duration is 2 hours and 15 minutes. The marking scheme is +4 marks for correct attempts and **-1 mark** for incorrect ones.

Subject Section No. of MCQs Important Core Topics
Technical English 50 Questions Grammar, Sentence Completion, Technical Writing, Vocabulary (Must Pass Section)
Engineering Mathematics 50 Questions Matrices, Integral & Differential Calculus, Solid Geometry, Fourier Series
Engineering Mechanics 50 Questions Vector Forces, Friction, Projectiles, Circular Motion, Work, Power & Energy

CRITICAL PASS RULE: Candidates must score a minimum qualifying score in the **Technical English** section to have their Mathematics and Mechanics papers evaluated. Ranks are determined solely based on Math and Mechanics marks, but English is a mandatory gateway!

4. Top Engineering Colleges accepts BCECE-LE in Bihar

Based on previous cutoffs, the leading government institutions in Bihar are:

  1. MIT Muzaffarpur (Muzaffarpur Institute of Technology) — Prestigious state brand, top placements.
  2. BCE Bhagalpur (Bhagalpur College of Engineering) — Excellent, highly sought-after engineering departments.
  3. NCE Chandi (Nalanda College of Engineering) — Reputed autonomous state campus.
  4. DCE Darbhanga (Darbhanga College of Engineering) — High core placements.
  5. GEC Gaya (Government Engineering College, Gaya) — Modern infrastructure and active academic circles.

5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the English qualifying score?
Historically, candidates must get at least 35% of marks in Technical English (approx 18-20 correct answers without negative offsets) to qualify for general evaluation. Focus heavily on basic technical vocabulary and grammar.
Is SBTE Bihar diploma mandatory?
No. Candidates holding a 3-year diploma from any AICTE-approved board in India can apply, provided they satisfy Bihar domicile or parent residency parameters.
