1. What is the Arunachal APJEE Lateral Entry Exam?

The **Arunachal Pradesh Joint Entrance Examination (APJEE)** is conducted by the **Directorate of Higher and Technical Education (DHTE), Government of Arunachal Pradesh**, in Itanagar.

APJEE is the primary entrance channel for state-domiciled engineering diploma holders to gain direct second-year (third semester) B.Tech admissions. APJEE ranks dictate allocations in state engineering quotas, NERIST, and multiple northeast institutions participating in the joint counseling rounds.

2. Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must satisfy these parameters to qualify for APJEE lateral entry:

  • Academic Qualification: Must have passed an AICTE-approved 3-year minimum Diploma course in Engineering or Technology from a recognized institution with at least 45% aggregate marks (40% for ST candidates).
  • Nativity: Placements in reserved seats are open strictly to permanent residents of Arunachal Pradesh belonging to native Scheduled Tribes (APST category) or government children of state employees.
  • Physical Fitness: Candidates must hold valid medical certificates of physical fitness to undergo practical laboratory trainings in engineering courses.

4. Top Engineering Colleges in Arunachal Pradesh

The leading engineering institutions participating in APJEE lateral entry selections are:

  1. NERIST Nirjuli (North Eastern Regional Institute of Science and Technology) — Centrally Funded Technical Institute, legendary northeast academic brand.
  2. NIT Arunachal Pradesh — Premium national institute (admits lateral entries via distinct centralized central lists or spot channels).
  3. RGU Itanagar (Rajiv Gandhi University) — Excellent infrastructure and central university departments.
  4. Tom Riba Institute — Leading state colleges for technical and science branches.

5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is NERIST admission governed entirely by APJEE?
No. NERIST conducts its own entrance exam (NEE-II/NEE-III) for direct entry of certificate/diploma holders into their modular degree systems. However, a specific state-quota seat matrix in NERIST is allocated based on APJEE ranks.
Where are the exam centers for APJEE?
The APJEE offline exam is conducted across designated municipal school and college hubs in major Arunachal cities, primarily Itanagar, Naharlagun, Pasighat, and Ziro.
