Centralized 2026 Calendar

Because each state entrance board (like **KEA, DTE Kerala, BCECEB, or TGCHE**) operates completely independently, tracking application deadlines and counseling windows can be highly confusing. A single missed date can cost you a full academic year.

This centralized dashboard tracks the latest schedules and exam dates across the primary state boards.

Major Changes Implemented in 2026

Keep these changes in mind for the current academic session:

  • Offline to CBT Transitions: Standard boards (such as Gujarat's DDCET and Odisha's OJEE) have fully transitioned from traditional pen-and-paper OMR sheets to online Computer Based Tests (CBT). Adjust your preparation strategy accordingly.
  • Branch-Mapping Relaxations: To align with the National Education Policy (NEP 2020), multiple universities (including VTU) have relaxed their branch alignment guidelines. Diploma holders from allied fields can now seek admission in core computing streams under specific bridging provisions.
  • Category Normalization: State reserves are enforcing centralized verification of SC/ST and OBC domicile credentials prior to online option lockings. Ensure all certificate formats match the official models.
