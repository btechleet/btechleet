Privacy and Security

Privacy Policy

At BTechLeet.com, we prioritize your trust and transparency. Learn what information we collect, how cookies are managed, and your rights as a visitor.

Effective Date: June 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We operate under a privacy-first model. Because BTechLeet.com is a static site built entirely on client-side frameworks, we do not deploy backend database storage or force mandatory user login.

  • Voluntary Data: Information like your name, email, or message details that you choose to submit via our Contact form. We only use this to respond directly to your queries.
  • Local Storage: Our interactive calculators (CGPA and percentage estimators) use local browser cache (LocalStorage) purely on your local machine to preserve your score metrics between sessions. This data is never transmitted to us or any backend system.
  • Log Files: Like most standard websites, we analyze server-level log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser brand, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and clicks. This data is entirely aggregated and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

2. Google AdSense & Cookie Compliance

To support the free education resources we build, we integrate Google AdSense advertising.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to BTechLeet.com or other web locations.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the official Google Ads Settings Page.

GDPR / CCPA Note: You can manage, reject, or completely opt-out of cookies at any time via the cookie banner at the bottom of our pages or by modifying your individual browser's privacy controls.

3. Third-Party Analytics and Links

Our pages contain standard hyperlinked references to external government directories and state admission brochures (e.g. OJEE, DCET, JELET sites). Please note that we have no authority or control over the cookie collection or content models of those external third-party sites, and we recommend reading their respective policies upon redirection.

4. Children's Privacy Protection

We prioritize online security for youngsters and young technical students. BTechLeet.com does not knowingly record or maintain any personal data from individuals under the age of 13. If you believe your child has submitted personal details on our site, reach out immediately and we will scrub it from our mail registers.

5. Revisions and Contact Details

We occasionally update our privacy procedures to align with regional data collection legal shifts. Any revisions will instantly be marked at the top-strip of this page. For any privacy feedback, contact us at:

btechleet@gmail.com
