KEA Eligibility Rules

Most state entrance councils (like Karnataka KEA and Kerala DTE) evaluate candidate eligibility based on the cumulative aggregate score percentage of all diploma semesters combined. A minimum of 45% (40% for SC/ST/BC) is standard.
